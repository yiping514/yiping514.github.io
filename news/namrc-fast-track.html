NAMRC 53 paper fast-tracked to the Journal of Manufacturing Systems

March 10, 2025

Yi-Ping's paper "Real-Time Decision-Making for Digital Twin in Additive Manufacturing with Model Predictive Control using Time-Series Deep Neural Networks" was considered high-quality work and recommended by the Scientific Committee of NAMRC 53 for fast-tracking to the Journal of Manufacturing Systems. Historically, fewer than 20 papers per year are fast-tracked — a great honor.
